The Vision of Future

Bangladesh's youth have long dreamed about transforming their country with Doraemon's cutting-edge technology, but they have shown that real change requires action rather than magic. Bangladesh, with lots of untapped potential, is currently seeing a revolution in its youth's enthusiasm and selfless work. Known as 'Bangladesh 2.0,' the voluntary works aim to represent resilience, community, and patriotism to make the surroundings beautiful. From cleaning the streets to mural painting, controlling traffic and maintaining security, young volunteers are making their dreams come true. They are bringing vibrancy, unity, and safety to the country.
